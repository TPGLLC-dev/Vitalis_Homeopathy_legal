Vitalis — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 16, 2026  •  Last Updated: May 16, 2026

The Penelope Group LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Vitalis mobile application ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Data Type When Collected Purpose
Email address Account registration Authentication, account recovery
Display name Google or Apple Sign-In Personalization (optional)

1.2 Information Generated by Your Use

Data Type When Collected Purpose
Analysis history When you run a remedy analysis (signed-in users only) Display your remedy history, enable PDF export
Selected symptoms Saved as part of analysis history Show which symptoms were part of each analysis
Matched remedies & scores Saved as part of analysis history Show analysis results in your history

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your device location
  • We do not collect your contacts, photos, or files
  • We do not use advertising trackers or analytics SDKs
  • We do not collect health data through Apple HealthKit or similar frameworks
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Store and display your remedy analysis history
  • Enable PDF export of your analysis results
  • Communicate with you about your account (e.g., password reset)

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication), hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure. Firebase provides encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.

Your analysis history is protected by Firestore security rules that ensure only you can read, create, or delete your own records. No other user can access your data.

While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Service providers: Google Firebase (authentication and data storage), Apple (Sign-In authentication), Google (Sign-In authentication). These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy policies.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access Your Data
Signed-in users can view all stored analysis history within the App's History tab at any time. Analysis history is only saved to our servers when you are signed in to an account.

5.2 Delete Your Account
You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen. Account deletion permanently removes your authentication credentials and all analysis history from our servers.

5.3 Use Without an Account
You can use the App's core features — symptom selection, remedy analysis, and Materia Medica browsing — without creating an account. When used without an account, no personal data or analysis results are stored on our servers.

5.4 Data Portability
Signed-in users can export individual analysis records as PDF files from the History tab.

5.5 Free and Pro Tiers
The free tier stores up to 3 analysis records. The Vitalis Pro upgrade (a one-time in-app purchase) provides unlimited analysis storage. In both cases, the data collected and stored is the same — the tier determines only the number of records retained, not the type of information collected.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Health Information

The symptoms you select and the remedy results generated are stored as part of your analysis history. This information is used solely to display your history within the App and is not shared with healthcare providers, insurance companies, or any third party. We do not use Apple HealthKit, Google Fit, or any health-data framework.

9. Data Retention

We retain your analysis history for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your authentication data and analysis history are permanently removed from our systems.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document and, where appropriate, through an in-app notice. Continued use of the App after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

If you are a California resident, you have the right to request disclosure of the categories of personal information we collect, the purposes for which it is used, and whether it is sold or shared. We do not sell your personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at the address below.

Our legal basis for processing is your consent (account creation) and legitimate interest (providing the App's functionality). To exercise your rights, contact us at the address below.
